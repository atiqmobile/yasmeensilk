Our Heritage

Five Decades of Uncompromising Quality

Founded in 1970 in Mumbai, Yasmeen Silk Corporation has grown from a family workshop into one of India's most trusted names in premium textiles. We specialise in hand-finished shawls, traditional keffiyehs, and intricately embroidered fabrics — all crafted with meticulous attention to detail and exported to discerning buyers across the Middle East, Africa, Europe, and beyond.
